Notes:

  • Item x will begin producing item x+1 at a rate of Math.floor( count-x / BASE^(level+2) ).
    eg. 100 bits (level 0) will produce 1 (100/10^2) part per second.
    1000 parts (level 1) will produce 1 (1000/10^3)block per second.
  • Prestige increases rate but doesn't increase the cost.
